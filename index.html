Quick start

✕
Contour finds the trail segments you've ridden more than once and ranks every attempt fastest-first — your own private leaderboard.
  1. Add rides. Drop .gpx files onto the Rides panel (or tap to choose — multiple is fine). No files handy? Hit Demo rides to explore with sample data.
  2. Segments detect themselves. Any stretch ridden on 2+ rides is matched automatically as you add rides — beat your best and you'll get a 🎉 PR alert. Tweak Match radius, Min length, or Min rides and press Detect segments to re-run.
  3. Track your progress. Open a segment for your PR, latest run vs your best, a time trend, and every attempt ranked. Click a ride's name for full ride stats.
  4. Split (optional). Open a segment, choose Split into sub-segments, and click the highlighted trail to drop cuts — each part gets its own ranking.
  5. Units. Toggle Metric / Imperial in the top bar anytime.
  6. Back up (optional). Add a GitHub token, then Back up now to save all your rides and segments to the repo (or turn on Auto-backup). Restore brings everything back on a new device or after clearing your browser.

Quick start

✕
Contour finds the trail segments you've ridden more than once and ranks every attempt fastest-first — your own private leaderboard.
  1. Add rides. Drop .gpx files onto the Rides panel (or tap to choose — multiple is fine). No files handy? Hit Demo rides to explore with sample data.
  2. Detect segments. Tweak Match radius, Min length, and Min rides if you like, then press Detect segments. Any stretch ridden on 2+ rides is matched automatically.
  3. Explore. Click a segment for its leaderboard, elevation profile, and start/finish pins on the map. Click a ride's name for full ride stats.
  4. Split (optional). Open a segment, choose Split into sub-segments, and click the highlighted trail to drop cuts — each part gets its own ranking.
  5. Units. Toggle Metric / Imperial in the top bar anytime.
  6. Share (optional). Add a GitHub token, pick your rider name, then Save rides / Save segments to sync through the repo. Friends press Reload all to pull everyone's rides.
